/// About Firm

Building AI infrastructure for Africa's energy future.

Kisima Consulting is a technical intelligence firm specializing in subsurface applications of artificial intelligence. We transform complex subsurface data and technical documents into interactive AI systems that accelerate decision-making for Africa's oil, gas & geothermal sector. Our work spans the continent but centers on East Africa; from Kenya's emerging South Lokichar oil fields to Uganda's Lake Albert development to Tanzania's offshore prospects. We combine petroleum engineering fundamentals with machine learning, natural language processing and agentic AI systems to solve real industry challenges.

We're engineers building the technical infrastructure for how Africa's energy sector will operate in the AI era.

Engineering Outcomes

description

Document Intelligence

The Problem:

Field Development Plans, reservoir studies, and regulatory documents are 300+ pages of dense technical content. Engineers spend hours searching for specific data instead of making informed decisions.

The Outcome:

Interactive AI systems that answer questions instantly with source citations. Transform static PDFs into queryable knowledge bases using NotebookLM and custom LLMs.

layers

Reservoir Intelligence

The Problem:

Manual formation analysis is time-consuming and subjective. Decision making needs to incorporate patterns within big data to aid engineers in decision making

The Outcome:

Machine learning models that classify lithologies from well logs with 85%+ accuracy. Automated formation evaluation at scale.

Agentic Systems for Technical Documents

The Problem:

Well completion reports, DSTs, and production data scattered across hundreds of PDFs. Knowledge locked in unstructured documents.

The Outcome:

Retrieval-Augmented Generation systems that synthesize information across multiple wells. Query entire fields and individual reports.
